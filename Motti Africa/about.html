About Us


Motii Africa is a startup company founded in March 2022 by a team of college friends with a passion for technology and the automotive industry. Our mission is to make it easier for people to buy and sell new and used cars, as well as find the spare parts they need.

We believe that technology has the power to transform the way people buy and sell cars, and we are dedicated to bringing this transformation to the African market. Our platform combines the convenience and speed of online shopping with the security and reliability of traditional car dealerships, making it easy for people to find the perfect vehicle or spare parts for their needs.

In the future, we hope to expand our services to include a range of innovative solutions for the automotive industry, including but not limited to financing options and maintenance services. Our goal is to become the go-to destination for all things automotive in Africa, and we are excited to see where this journey takes us.
